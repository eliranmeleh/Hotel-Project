The Queen of Shva Eilat Hotel is in the heart of the city of Eilat, in a place where the Red Sea and the desert mountains enjoy the warm sun.

The hotel, which was inspired by the biblical palace of the Queen of Sheba, offers its guests all the possible means of comfort, in a luxurious and stunningly beautiful building.

The hotel offers 481 beautiful and furnished rooms, luxurious suites with an outdoor jacuzzi facing the sea and rooms adapted exactly for families, a view of the Red Sea bay, the romantic lagoon and the desert mountains.

The Queen of Shva Eilat Hotel Promenade offers a variety of shops and an entertainment center for the whole family.
